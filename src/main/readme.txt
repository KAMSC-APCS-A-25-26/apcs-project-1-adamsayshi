You are a contestant on Love Island located in Australia, hoping to find the love of your life.
However, your mom is incredibly disappointed that you have to go on a dating show to find a
girlfriend. You ignore her and continue on with the show, with tears in your eyes. She kicked
you out the house, so you better find a perfect girlfriend who will be your sugar mommy. This
game will guide you through your life, and it will teach you the cost benefit analysis of
various options and teach you several life lessons along the way.
IMPORTANT: The options will have the valid options in the parenthesis, type one of them
precisely. If you type in something unknown, it will default to the second option.
